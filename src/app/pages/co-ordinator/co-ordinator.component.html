OUR COORDINATORS  


Coming together is beginning, keeping together is progress and working together is a success. We at Gyanarpan work with students from different colleges. These students act as coordinators, work closely with other students, and help in painting pictures of tomorrow. They oversee and manage every small to large essential aspects of technical activities. All coordinators are responsible for bridging the gap between industry and colleges. Each coordinator on this portal has the potential to learn, create, innovate and flourish.
